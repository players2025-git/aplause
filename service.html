Maternity & Obstetric Care

Care for mothers before, during, and after delivery — personalized, safe, and compassionate.

  • Antenatal & Postnatal Care

    Comprehensive checkups, nutrition counselling and continuous monitoring for mother and baby.

  • Normal Delivery & Caesarean Section (CS)

    Expert obstetricians, modern theaters and attentive midwifery support.

  • High-risk pregnancy management

    Specialized monitoring, maternal-fetal medicine consultations, and tailored birth plans.

Gynecological & Surgical Procedures

Advanced surgical and gynecologic treatments performed with precision and empathy.

  • Fibroid Treatment (Myomectomy & Hysterectomy)

    Minimally invasive and open surgical options based on best practice.

  • Laparoscopic Procedures

    Faster recovery and minimal scarring using keyhole techniques.

  • Colposcopy & Cervical Procedures

    Diagnostic and therapeutic care for cervical abnormalities.

Reproductive & Fertility Care

Personalized solutions for conception and fertility, backed by modern diagnostics.

  • Infertility Evaluation & Treatment

    Comprehensive testing and individualized treatment pathways.

  • Intrauterine Insemination (IUI)

    When appropriate, supported by monitoring and counseling.

  • Fertility Preservation

    Options and guidance for egg freezing and family planning.

Preventive & Wellness Services

Screenings and education for lifelong women’s health — prevention first.

  • Well-woman Screenings

    Pap smears, breast checks, and routine health assessments.

  • Family Planning & Contraceptive Services

    Counseling and a full range of contraceptive options.

  • Health Education Workshops

    Community programs on nutrition, breastfeeding, and postpartum mental health.
